About Me

Hi, I’m Wbh Rafi, a passionate photographer with a deep love for capturing raw, real, and timeless moments. Specializing in street and wedding documentation, my journey behind the lens began in the wedding industry in 2019, and since then, photography has become more than just a profession—it’s my way of telling stories.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with some incredible teams, including K. Nasif Photography (2019–2022) and Aynamahal (2023–2025). These experiences have shaped my perspective, taught me the value of authenticity, and sharpened my eye for detail—especially in capturing candid moments, group interactions, and expressive portraits, both in indoor and outdoor settings.

Alongside my professional work, I also run a small creative farm called রঙ্গচিত্র, where I explore personal projects and creative ideas that continue to inspire my craft.

Looking ahead, my goal is to establish myself as one of the best in the field, leaving a mark through honest storytelling and meaningful visuals that stand the test of time.
